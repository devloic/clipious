<i>Clipious</i> is a client for Invidious, the privacy focused YouTube front end.
<b>Features:</b>
<ul>
<li>Use own or public server</li>
<li>Subscription management</li>
<li>SponsorBlock + DeArrow</li>
<li>Video view/progress tracking</li>
<li>User playlists</li>
<li>Background playback</li>
<li>Live stream support</li>
<li>Android TV UI</li>
<li>Audio playback</li>
<li>Video and audio download</li>
<li>Video filtering</li>
<li>Return Youtube Dislikes</li>
</ul>
